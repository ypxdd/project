FAQ


Question: What are the rental rates?

Toy Outpost offers one of the most competitive pricings for our lockers. Our rental rates varies within the different outlets and according to their positions. Kindly walk in to any of our outlets to approach our friendly staff for availability and pricing.

Question: What are the items that can't be sold?

Toy Outpost takes a firm stance against intellectual property rights and copy rights infringements. We do however allow merchants to place authentic and licensed products for sales in our locker boxes. Merchandise that requires approval, license or notification from a local Authority / Department must be obtained first before being allowed for distribution sale in the Company.

Question: What is the minimum age for renting lockers?

Only those that are above 21 is allowed to rent a locker with us. For young merchants below 21, kindly sign up legal guardian or parent to sign on the locker on behalf.
